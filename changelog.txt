Hello, this is Benny/Sanio. You might recognize that name from my work on the Eevee character mod, Epiphany, or other projects. Arachna had a lot of charm to them and clearly caught the attention of many people during the early days of Repentance, but unfortuantely has been weighed down from its lack of updates and increasing amounts of bugs. I'm a sucker for abandoned character-related mods so I left a comment and was able to get in contact with rvsty for permission to revive the mod!

**PLEASE READ!**
Now, an important note regarding this update: Arachna now requires REPENTOGON on Repentance+. This decision was OKAY'd by rvsty and is honestly the best route for continued support on the mod. The biggest reason for this is for an easier time rebuilding the mod's codebase, fixing countless bugs through more natural implementation of various mechanics, and less upkeep required with only one version (RGON+) to maintain.

General Changes/Fixes:
- The amount of bugs fixed as a direct result of the switch to REPENTOGON is difficult to account for, but hopefully there should generally be less bugs and more improvements across the whole mod!
- The utility of throwing Arachna's Spool now utilizes ThrowableItemLib for general improvements and cross-MOD compatibility
- Web Hearts now utilize CustomHealthAPI for general improvements and cross-MOD compatibility
- Tainted Arachna no longer uses a manually handled item wisp of Mutant Spider, now instead having a natural integration of the multishot. This also allows their multishot to be utilized in the Mines chase sequence
- Fixed sparkles from Arachna's Golden Spiders not being removed properly, causing increasing amounts of lag over time until you left the room
- Rainbow and Golden Spiders now change the color of their glow to match their own color
- Fixed the Spider Egg orbital from Arachnid's Grip not having a variant assigned, which had self-assigned itself a variant of 0
- Achievements and Geptameron's Giantbook are now displayed using REPENTOGON's own implementation instead of GiantBookAPI
- Lil Arachna is now a more naturally integrated shooter familiar and should obey all shooter familiar interactions
- Poison tears from Arachnid's Grip are colored green

Gameplay Changes:
- Arachna and Tainted Arachna's base stats are now properly integrated as base stats similar to vanilla and respect damage/firerate multipliers
- As a result of the above, Tainted Arachna's firerate down is now closer to how it works for the Keepers, allowing you to reach the normal cap of 5.00 tears per second
- Arachna and Tainted Arachna no longer get slowed from cobwebs on the ground
- Web Hearts replacing specific heart types will now only occur if every player is either Arachna or Tainted Arachna, not counting "Strawman" players, instead of if any single character was Arachna or Tainted Arachna
- The above change is also reflected for Web Hearts turning into blue spiders for any "Keeper" characters
- 3D Glasses now works for all weapon types and has better optimization for how it renders its 3D effect
- 3D Glasses now copies the champion color of the enemy for its 3D copies
- Infested Penny's chance to spawn a Web Heart now scales with both the value of the coin and trinket multipliers

API Changes:
- Everything realted to the mod is now exposed through the ARACHNAMOD global. No more magic numbers!
